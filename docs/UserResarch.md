Observation 1: Weather Feature

Observation Statement: Weather-based outfit recommendations is the most universally desired feature.

Supporting Data: It received the highest concentration of "Very useful" and "Extremely useful" ratings across all age groups and demographics

Team Action (if any): Prioritize weather integration as a core feature in the app


Observation 2: The "Onboarding Wall"

Observation Statement: Manual setup is the primary barrier to user retention and long-term engagement.

Supporting Data: 49.2% of users cited "Too much setup (taking photos, tagging items)" as the top reason they would stop using the app. 51.7% of respondents are only willing to spend 10 minutes or less setting up their digital closet.

Team Action (if any): Implement "Bulk Upload" or AI-assisted auto-tagging (identifying color/type from a photo automatically) to minimize manual data entry during the initial setup phase.


Observation 3: Wardrobe Management & Sustainability

Observation Statement: There is significant user interest in tracking clothing "utility", specifically what is being worn versus what is being ignored.

Supporting Data: Open ended responses specifically highlighted a desire to "track how much of your wardrobe you actually wear" and "give data on which clothes you wear most frequently.

Team Action (if any): Potentially develop a "Wardrobe Insights" dashboard that tracks the frequency of use for each item.
